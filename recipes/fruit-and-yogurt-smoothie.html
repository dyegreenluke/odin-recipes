Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

"I love it!"

A simple, delicious, and flexible recipe for a fruit and yogurt smoothie. Ingredients are substitutable.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Abracadabra! Pour into a glass or drink straight from the blender!
