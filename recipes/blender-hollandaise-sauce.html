Blender Hollandaise Sauce

"Refreshing!"

Hollandaise sauce, made convenient and easy by using a blender.

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Combine egg yolks, lemon juice, mustard, and hot pepper sauce in the container of a blender.
  2. Cover and blend for about 5 seconds.
  3. Place butter in a glass measuring cup. Heat butter in the microwave for about 1 minute, or until hot and completely melted.
  4. Turn the blender on; pour melted butter in a slow, steady stream until sauce is thick and smooth, about 15 to 30 seconds.
  5. Presto! Your hollandaise sauce is ready to be served.
